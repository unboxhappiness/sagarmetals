Common Questions

Frequently
Asked Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us directly and our team will assist you.

Ask a Question
How do you determine the scrap price?
+
Our prices are based on live commodity exchange rates (LME, MCX) and are updated daily. We offer competitive rates well above the local market. Call us for the day's confirmed rate before bringing your scrap.
Do you provide pickup service?
+
Yes, we provide free pickup for bulk quantities. Our vehicle, labor, and equipment are included at no charge. Pickup is typically arranged within 24 hours of confirmation.
What is the minimum quantity for collection?
+
For free pickup, we generally require a minimum of 200–500 kg depending on the metal type. For smaller quantities, you can deliver directly to our yard. Contact us and we'll accommodate.
How and when do you make payment?
+
Payment is made immediately after weighing — via NEFT/RTGS, UPI, or cash. We provide a proper GST invoice and weight slip for every transaction.
Do you buy mixed or unsorted scrap?
+
Yes. We accept mixed, unsorted, and contaminated scrap. Our team will sort and assess on-site, and you'll receive a fair composite rate for the entire lot.
Can you handle large industrial dismantling projects?
+
Absolutely. We have experience in complete plant dismantling and machinery disposal projects. We can provide a project team, logistics support, and advance payment arrangements for large volumes.
